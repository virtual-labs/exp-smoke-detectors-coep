PROCEDURE

Prerequisite
  1. Read and understand all instructions and questions provided
  2. Answer all questions and click on the "Submit Test" button
  3. Upon successful completion, click on the "Next Level" button which will appear in place of the "Submit" button
Simulator
  1. Select frequency and source of sound
  2. Click on "MEASUREMENT OF SOUND LEVEL" button
  3. Click on "START" button and observe dB level of sound
  4. Click on STOP button
  5. Enter the sound level reading(dB) in textbox and click on Submit button
  6. Change the source of sound and follow the above steps
  7. Click on "FINISH" button
